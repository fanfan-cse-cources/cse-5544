DinoFun World App

The DinoFun World app is your electronic ticket to all the fun that awaits at DinoFun World!

  • Use the app just like your ticket anywhere in the park.
  • Check in at rides for instant updates on wait times
  • Text with your friends in the park for free!
  • Install it on your phone, or borrow a device from the park.

 

Play the Cindysaurus Trivia Game!

  • The Cindysaurus Trivia Game tests your knowledge about the history, attractions, and entertainment at DinoFun World.
  • Test your Cindysaurus knowledge against family and friends!
  • Win fabulous prizes!
  • Hours of fun!

 

The Cindysaurus Trivia Game is available from the DinoFun World app. No in-app purchase required.
