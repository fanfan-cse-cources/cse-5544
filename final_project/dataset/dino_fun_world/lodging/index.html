Lodging

Jeredactyl Lodge

Jeredactyl Lodge is conveniently located next to DinoFun World.

  • The Lodge offers modern accommodations with a Jurassic feel! Be surrounded by giant ferns and friendly raptors!
  • Visit our award-winning Dino-Diner in the Main Lodge
  • Travel back in time to share a meal with a giant-sized friend!

 

Camp Iguanodon

Conveniently located next to DinoFun World is the Camp Iguanodon.

  • Come enjoy beautiful, shaded areas with convenient pull-through sites
  • Recreational vehicles and tents welcome!
